A chameleon is a type of old world lizard from the family Chamaeleonidae, well-known for its ability to change color. This color change is used for communication and temperature regulation, not just camouflage. Other distinct characteristics include independently rotating eyes, a long and rapid-protruding tongue, prehensile tail, and unique, tongs-like feet. The term "chameleon" is also used metaphorically for a person who frequently changes their opinions or behavior to blend in with different groups.
